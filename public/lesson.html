Grammar

expand_more

Using 叫 (jiào)

The verb 叫 (jiào) means "to be called". Use the pattern: Subject + 叫 + Name

我叫李明。

Wǒ jiào Lǐ Míng.

I am called Li Ming.

Particle 呢 (ne)

The particle 呢 (ne) is used at the end of a question to ask "what about...?" or "where is...?"

我叫李明。你呢？

Wǒ jiào Lǐ Míng. Nǐ ne?

I'm called Li Ming. And you?

warning Note: 呢 is always at the end.
